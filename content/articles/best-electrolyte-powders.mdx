Electrolyte powders all promise the same thing — better hydration — but they vary a
lot in sodium content, added sugar, and price. This roundup compares them on the
factors that actually matter.

## How we compared them

We looked at four things: **sodium per serving** (the electrolyte most people
actually need during heavy sweat), **added sugar**, whether the product is
**third-party tested**, and **cost per serving**. Ratings reflect those factors,
not commission rates.

> Prices shown are as of the update date and may vary.

## Who needs more electrolytes?

For most people doing light activity, water and a normal diet are enough.
Electrolyte powders may support hydration for people who sweat heavily, train in
heat, or are very active. They are not a treatment for any medical condition — if
you're managing one, talk to your clinician.
